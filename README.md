# Chameleon ~ AI Public Health & Nutrition Mobile Application

The “Chameleon” application is a service that people of all age types and needs can use to personalize their body goals in a quick and effective manner. 

Looking for nutrition tips online can take hours, several videos, several websites, and overall is time consuming to figure out what a specific person actually needs for their own body type and structure. Because every human requires specific needs that differ from person to person, the app uses AI to research and evaluate the user to inform them of the most effective decisions to improve their health and their lifestyle. 

The AI takes key information that the user puts in, such as weight, age, height, and other important statistics such as eating habits, diets, pre-existing health conditions, and exercise.

It uses this information to make a customized plan to adhere to the goals the user indicated and provides links to the resources if necessary for more information. By receiving this information almost instantly, the user saves time and stress instead of looking at long videos and searching the web extensively for tips that might work. However, there are limitations to the development of this application up until now. This includes the absence of professional input which could be used to more accurately collect and calculate the correct information needed, andThe purpose of this app is thus to provide an efficient way to coalesce all the information a user may need to best meet their health and nutritional needs.
